# Content Plan

## First Topic Cluster

Topic: E-commerce email and SMS marketing tools.

Audience:

- Shopify and DTC store owners comparing retention tools.
- Small marketing teams setting up abandoned cart, welcome, post-purchase, and winback flows.
- Stores deciding whether SMS should live inside an email platform or a dedicated SMS tool.

Primary pages:

- `/categories/email-marketing-tools`
- `/best/best-email-marketing-tools-for-ecommerce`
- `/tools/klaviyo`
- `/tools/omnisend`
- `/tools/postscript`
- `/compare/klaviyo-vs-omnisend`
- `/alternatives/klaviyo-alternatives`

Search intent map:

- Category page: broad discovery for e-commerce email marketing tools.
- Best page: ranked shortlist for buyers comparing options.
- Tool pages: product review, pricing fit, pros, cons, and alternatives.
- Comparison page: direct decision between Klaviyo and Omnisend.
- Alternatives page: replacement options for users who already know Klaviyo.

Current content status:

- Seed data includes stronger editorial summaries for Klaviyo, Omnisend, and Postscript.
- Best list, comparison, and alternatives pages include more specific criteria and verdicts.
- Source URLs are stored for official product and help pages.
- Pricing text intentionally avoids hard-coded prices because plan limits and usage billing can change.
